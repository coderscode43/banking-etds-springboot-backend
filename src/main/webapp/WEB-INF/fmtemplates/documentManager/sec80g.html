Investments Declaration/Section 80G- Donation

The deduction under section 80G is available in respect of donations made by you towards certain specified funds, charitable institutions etc.

For claiming donation under this section following conditions must be fulfilled: The donation should be made in any mode of payment other than cash if it exceeds Rs. 10,000.Donations in kind are not eligible for deduction under this section. The donation should be made only to specified funds or institutions.
