Investments Declaration/Section 80GGA- Donation for scientific research(35AC institution)

If you make any donation for Scientific Research or Rural Development, then you can avail deduction under this section. Under Sec 80GGA deduction, the whole amount of donation is allowed as deduction without any upper limit. However, cash donations of more than Rs. 10,000 are not allowed under this section.
